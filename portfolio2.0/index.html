03 — ABOUT

Me (right) and Lama (left) — 4000+m high in northern Argentina

Hi, my name is Daniel. I'm almost 18 years old, currently living in a small village in Salta, northern Argentina — though usually I'm from Munich, Germany. I speak conversational Spanish, good English and German.

I like volleyball, table tennis, football. I love driving — motorbikes, fast boats, anything with a throttle. I love travelling and seeing the world. And obviously: designing and creating things.

About this site. The first version was inspired by the original Apple Macintosh — the Hello, the rainbow logo, the serif font. For this version I went the opposite direction: dark, typographic, confident. Same love for design, different mood entirely.

On designing. My passion might trace back to an Apple TV series called "Home" — Episode 3, Bali, bamboo houses. That episode made me want to become an architect. The urge to build beautiful things stayed. It just became digital.
