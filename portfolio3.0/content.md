### About this website:
My idea or concept with this Website was inspired by the first Apple Macinthosch. The Hello, the logo with the stripes (rainbow), the font, it´s all kind of stolen from apple. I am personely honestly not 100% happy with the design of the page, I like that it is diffrent and Unique, and ofcourse the old-apple-design inspiration, but there are a lot of things that could be better. Nontheless I just wanted to stop waiting to find/make the perfect design and just code the page so I have something to showcase. So maybe in some time I will find the time and energy for redoing this, until then this is it, I hope you at least like it a little bit. Eventhough it´s such a small page, coding it (especially the stupid shap of the main-container) was quite some work coding it, especially because I am not using some no-code website-buidling tools like framer.
